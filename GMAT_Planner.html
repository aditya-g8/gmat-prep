How the plan is built

First 30% of your weeks: fundamentals & concept building. Next 40%: section-by-section practice. Final 30%: full-length mocks & error review. Your 6 free GMAC official mocks are spaced from the end of the fundamentals phase to the week before the exam. Section time follows GMAT Focus weightage — all three sections count equally on the exam, with slightly more prep time on Quant for its larger syllabus: Quant 40% · Verbal 30% · Data Insights 30%.
